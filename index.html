Digital Payments — Electronic Revenue Collection (ERC)

A comprehensive platform for collecting fees, taxes, and customs across channels with unified payments and global standards.

ERC automates revenue collection processes end to end and unifies payments so governments can collaborate to serve society efficiently. Fees may be collected via various channels and payment methods, with full auditability and real‑time reporting.

  • Unified collection for fees, taxes, customs
  • Multi‑channel payment support and reconciliation
  • Real‑time analytics, dashboards, and fraud monitoring
  • Standards‑based integrations with existing systems
